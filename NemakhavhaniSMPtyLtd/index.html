About Us



Who Are We?


We as Nemakhavhani's SM Contractors are a construction collective that strives to improve the lives of people by building them homes as well as maintaining them. We Seek to eradicate homelessness. We love the art of construction and we are very much goal and hard work oriented.



What Do We Do?


Our primary specialty is that we build houses for our clients but our business not restricted.We also provide services such as Plastering, Painting, Roofing Plumbing, Building(all brick types) Screed, Electric wiring, Sledge maintaining and as well as home renovations.













Our Services



Plumbing



From Pipes, Tanks and all the way to other apparatus required for the water supply and sanitation in a building, We install and maintain plumbing systems.





Painting

Construction
